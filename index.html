AB
the game
How to play this game?

The goal of this game is to guess a 4-digit number within the least number of attempts possible.

With every guess, you will get a feedback indicating how many A and B you got with the guess.

An A means: one of the digits is correct, and is also at the right place.

A B means: one of the digits is a right number, but not at the right place.

For example, given that the answer is 0130, a guess of 3610 will receive 1A2B. 1A is 0, 2B are 1 and 3.

Ready? Start!

Press [enter] to guess.
